Description:
Abstract:
The Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) Caribbean VFR Aeronautical Raster Chart series is designed to meet the needs of users who require georeferenced raster images of FAA Visual Flight Rules (VFR) Caribbean VFR Aeronautical Charts (WACs). A Caribbean VFR Aeronautical Raster Chart is a raster image of an FAA WAC. The image inside the neat line is georeferenced to the surface of the earth. Only the main body of the chart is accurately georeferenced. The raster data may be viewed using a geographical information system to take advantage of the georeferenced nature of the data or the data can be viewed using commonly available image viewing software.
Purpose:
The Caribbean VFR Aeronautical Raster Chart series has been developed to meet the needs of many users. The data has the flexibility of being useful for both industry and individuals. Additional digital data may easily be overlaid on the raster image. Data such as weather, temporary flight restrictions, obstacles, or other geospatial data can be combined with Caribbean VFR Aeronautical Raster Chart data to support a variety of needs.
Supplemental_Information:
Each Caribbean VFR Aeronautical Raster Chart is provided as one file. Each file name is structured by chart name and edition number, e.g., CAC-1_1.tif. The files are 300 dots per inch and 8-bit color. The data is provided as a GeoTIFF.
